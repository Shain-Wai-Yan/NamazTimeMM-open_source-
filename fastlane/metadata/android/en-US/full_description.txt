Namaz Time MM is a privacy-focused Islamic prayer times calculator specifically optimized for Myanmar.

Features:
- Precise solar calculation for 20+ Myanmar cities.
- Global coverage with multiple calculation methods (MWL, Karachi, ISNA, etc.).
- Bilingual support: Burmese and English interfaces.
- Hijri calendar integration.
- Qibla direction finder.
- Completely offline functionality after initial setup.
- 100% Free and Open Source with NO tracking or analytics.
